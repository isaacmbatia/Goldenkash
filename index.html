FAQ

Common Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before you start your online income journey.

Get Started Free →
Can I really make money online? +

Yes — through freelancing, affiliate marketing, YouTube, online courses, and AI tools. Results depend on consistency and strategy, but thousands of people worldwide build full-time income online every year.

How much can I realistically earn? +

Beginners typically earn $50–$200/month in the first 1–3 months. Part-time earners reach $300–$1,000/month, while full-time online entrepreneurs often earn $2,000+/month.

Do I need experience or a degree? +

No experience or degree required. Our step-by-step guides walk you through everything from scratch. The only requirement is consistency and willingness to learn.

Can I make money online from Kenya? +

Absolutely. Platforms like Fiverr, Upwork, YouTube, Amazon, and Shopify are fully accessible to Kenyan users. Payments are received via M-Pesa, PayPal, Payoneer, and bank transfer.

Do I need to invest money to start? +

Many methods — like freelancing, affiliate marketing, and YouTube — are 100% free to start. Others, like dropshipping or Amazon FBA, benefit from a small starting budget of $50–$300.

How long before I see real income? +

Quick methods like freelancing can generate income within days. Content-based income (YouTube, blogging, affiliate) typically takes 3–6 months to build meaningful and sustainable earnings.
