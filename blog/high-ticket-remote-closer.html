How to Become a High-Ticket Remote Closer (Earn $10k+ Commissions)

✍️
GoldenKash Editorial Team
March 2026 · 11 min read

The creator economy is booming. Influencers, coaches, and consultants are launching $5,000 Masterminds and $10,000 business coaching programs. But there is a massive problem: you cannot sell a $10,000 product with a simple "Buy Now" button. People need to speak to a human before dropping that kind of money.

These influencers don't have time to take 10 sales calls a day. So, they hire High-Ticket Remote Closers. You take the calls, sell the program over Zoom or phone, and pocket a 10% to 15% commission on every sale.

💰 Remote Closer Math

If you sell a $10,000 fitness coaching program and earn a 10% commission ($1,000/sale). If you take 4 calls a day (20/week), and close at a modest 25% rate (5 sales), you earn $5,000 a week. All from your laptop, with no fulfillment duties.

What Exactly is a Remote Closer?

A closer is not a cold-caller. You are not dialing random numbers out of a phone book. You deal strictly with "inbound warm leads." This means the prospective buyer has already watched a webinar, consumed the creator's content, and proactively booked a call on your calendar. Your job is to uncover their pain points and determine if the coaching program is the right fit to solve their problems.

Step 1: The Skills You Need to Learn

High-ticket sales is about psychology, not being pushy. In fact, the best closers sound like therapists.

  • Active Listening: 80% of the call is listening, 20% is asking the right questions.
  • Objection Handling: When a prospect says "I need to think about it" or "It's too expensive," you must gracefully navigate the hesitation without resorting to sleazy car-salesman tactics.
  • Consultative Selling: You position yourself as a doctor diagnosing a patient, rather than a vendor pushing a product.

Step 2: How to Find Influencers Who Need Closers

Do not apply on Indeed or LinkedIn; those are mostly low-ticket telemarketing jobs. The best way to land a high-ticket closing role is direct outreach on Instagram or Facebook.

  1. Find mid-sized creators (50k - 200k followers) in business, fitness, dating, or real estate niches.
  2. Click the link in their bio. If it leads to a "Book a Strategy Call" page, they have a high-ticket offer.
  3. Send a video pitch via Instagram DM or cold email: "Hey [Name], I noticed you're scaling your mastermind. I'm an experienced closer with a 35% close rate in the B2B space. Are your calendars currently maxed out, or do you have room for an aggressive remote closer to take 20 calls a week off your plate?"

Step 3: Acing the "Roleplay" Interview

An influencer will not hire you based on a resume. They will hop on a Zoom call and ask you to roleplay a mock sales scenario. They will pretend to be a difficult prospect breaking down with a money objection. You must demonstrate empathy, control of the conversation, and the ability to ask probing follow-up questions.

FAQ

Do I get a base salary? +

Rarely. 95% of high-ticket closing roles are 100% commission-based. This is terrifying for some, but it's why the earning potential is uncapped. If you want a safe $50k salary, do a traditional corporate SDR role. If you want a chance to make $250k working 5 hours a day, remote closing is the better path.
