20 Legit Ways to Make $100 Online

🌍 Geographic Note: While these strategies work globally, this guide is highly optimized for readers in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia looking to earn in USD/GBP.
✍️

Making your first $100 online is a significant milestone — it proves the concept works and builds confidence to scale up. The good news? There are dozens of legitimate methods, many of which can generate $100 within your very first week. Here are 20 that actually work in 2026.

💰 Income Range Per Method

Most methods below can realistically earn $50–$500 in your first month. Many can be combined for faster results.

The 20 Methods

1 Fiverr Freelancing

Create a gig on Fiverr offering a service — writing, logo design, data entry, proofreading, or voice-over. Even a simple $10 gig completed 10 times gets you to $100. Fiverr has millions of daily buyers looking for affordable freelancers.

2 Sell on Facebook Marketplace

Declutter your home or source items cheaply (thrift stores, garage sales) and resell them on Facebook Marketplace. Many people earn $100–$300/week flipping items they found for a fraction of the resale price.

3 Data Entry Jobs

Sites like Clickworker, Amazon Mechanical Turk, and Microworkers offer data entry micro-tasks. While not high-paying alone, they're accessible to anyone and can produce steady side income.

4 Sell Photos Online

Upload your smartphone photos to Shutterstock, Adobe Stock, or iStock and earn royalties every time someone downloads them. A portfolio of 50+ quality photos can generate $50–$200/month passively.

5 Online Surveys

Survey Junkie, Swagbucks, and Toluna pay for your opinions. Not a replacement income, but an easy $30–$100/month in spare time.

6 Transcription Services

Transcribe audio files into text via Rev.com or TranscribeMe. Pay is $0.45–$1.10 per minute of audio, or $22–$45/hour for experienced transcribers.

7 Sell Digital Templates

Design Canva templates, Notion dashboards, or Excel spreadsheets and sell them on Etsy or Gumroad. Templates sell repeatedly with zero ongoing effort.

8 Virtual Assistant Work

Manage emails, schedule appointments, and handle social media for small business owners. VAs earn $10–$25/hour on platforms like Zirtual, Time Etc, and Upwork.

9 Affiliate Marketing — Social Media

Join Amazon Associates or ClickBank, then share product links in your WhatsApp groups, Facebook, or TikTok. A single viral post can generate $100+ in commissions.

10 Teach English Online

Platforms like Cambly, Preply, and iTalki connect native or fluent English speakers with language learners. Earn $10–$25/hour from your phone or laptop.

11 Proofread & Edit Documents

Proofread student essays, business reports, and manuscripts. Proofread Anywhere and Upwork are good starting points. Rates: $15–$40/hour.

12 Social Media Management

Many small businesses need help running their Instagram, Facebook, or LinkedIn pages. Charge $100–$500/month per client to manage content and engagement.

13 YouTube Shorts / TikTok Content

Create short-form videos around trending topics. The TikTok Creator Fund and YouTube Shorts Monetization can start generating small income from as little as 10,000 views.

14 Drop Service

Find a client who needs a service (e.g., logo design), outsource it to a cheaper freelancer on Fiverr, and keep the margin. Many drop-service businesses earn $50–$200 per project.

15 Write Articles for Pay

Content mills like iWriter, Textbroker, and WriterAccess pay $5–$50 per article. Produce 2–5 articles per day and you'll hit $100 within a week.

16 Test Websites & Apps

UserTesting.com and TryMyUI pay $10–$15 per 20-minute test of websites and apps. Simply browse normally and share your thoughts aloud.

17 Sell Unused Domain Names

Register cheap domain names (like BestKenyaCafes.com for $10) and sell them on Flippa or Sedo for $50–$500+ to businesses seeking a strong domain.

18 Online Tutoring

Tutor students in maths, science, English, or coding via Wyzant, Chegg Tutors, or directly through WhatsApp at $15–$50/hour.

19 Print-on-Demand Products

Upload designs to Redbubble, Merch by Amazon, or Printful. When a customer buys a mug or T-shirt with your design, you earn a royalty — no inventory needed.

20 Resell on Amazon

Buy discounted products locally or online and resell them on Amazon FBA at a profit. Even with a $50 test budget, many resellers turn a $100 profit in their first month.

❌ Cons

FAQ

Which is the fastest way to make $100 online? +

UserTesting, freelance writing, and Fiverr are typically the fastest — you can complete tasks or gigs within 24–72 hours and receive payment within a week.

Can I do multiple methods at once? +

Yes, and it's a smart strategy. Many earners combine surveys + freelancing + affiliate links for layered income. Just avoid spreading yourself too thin at first.
