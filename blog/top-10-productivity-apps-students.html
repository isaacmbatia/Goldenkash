Top 10 Productivity Apps Every Student Should Try

✍️
GoldenKash Editorial Team
March 2026 · 7 min read

Welcome to our guide on the Top 10 Productivity Apps Every Student Should Try. Whether you're in high school or university, these apps will help you study smarter, stay organized, and even start earning while you learn.

Best Study & Note-Taking Apps

Notion for all-in-one organization. Anki for spaced repetition flashcards. Obsidian for linked note-taking. Forest for focus sessions. Google Calendar for time-blocking your study schedule.

Collaboration & Group Work Apps

Google Docs for real-time collaboration. Canva for group presentations. Slack for team communication. Miro for brainstorming and mind mapping. Grammarly for polishing essays and papers.

Apps That Help Students Earn

Fiverr for freelance gigs between classes. Notion for building and selling templates. Canva for creating sellable designs. ChatGPT for research assistance that speeds up content creation.
