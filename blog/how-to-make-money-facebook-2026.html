How to Make Money on Facebook in 2026: Complete Guide

✍️
GoldenKash Editorial Team
March 2026 · 10 min read

Welcome to our Complete Guide to Making Money on Facebook in 2026. With over 3 billion users, Facebook remains one of the most powerful platforms for generating income — if you know how to use it right.

Facebook Marketplace

Sell new or used items locally with zero listing fees. Resellers are making thousands flipping thrift store finds, wholesale goods, and handmade products through Marketplace.

Monetizing Facebook Pages

Grow a niche Facebook Page to 10k+ followers, then earn through in-stream ads, branded content, fan subscriptions, and Stars. Video content gets the highest ad revenue payouts.

Facebook Groups for Income

Build a paid community, sell digital products to group members, or use your group as a funnel for coaching, courses, and affiliate offers. Engaged groups are goldmines.

Facebook Ads for Business

Run ads for local businesses as a social media manager, or promote your own products and services. Facebook's ad platform remains one of the most powerful targeting tools available.
