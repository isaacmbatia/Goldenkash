What Is Dropshipping?

Dropshipping is an e-commerce model where you sell products without holding any inventory. When a customer orders from your store, the supplier ships directly to them. You keep the margin between your retail price and the supplier cost. Shopify makes building a dropshipping store straightforward even for beginners.

Step 1 – Set Up Your Shopify Store

  1. Go to shopify.com and start your free 3-day trial (then $1/month for 3 months)
  2. Choose a store name and URL
  3. Select a clean, conversion-focused theme (Dawn is free and excellent)
  4. Set up your payment processor — Shopify Payments or PayPal
  5. Configure shipping zones and rates

Step 2 – Find Winning Products

Use AutoDS, Zendrop, or DSers to connect to AliExpress suppliers. Look for products with: 100+ orders (proof of demand), positive reviews, ePacket shipping option, and a 30–100% markup potential. Winning dropshipping products in 2026 include: home decor, pet accessories, fitness gadgets, and eco-friendly products.

Step 3 – Drive Traffic

  • Facebook/Instagram Ads: Most effective for testing. Start with $10–$20/day per product.
  • TikTok Ads + Organic: Fastest-growing traffic source for e-commerce
  • Google Shopping Ads: High intent buyers
  • Influencer Marketing: DM micro-influencers for product promotions
  • Pinterest SEO: Free, long-term traffic for lifestyle products

Pros

  • Zero inventory risk
  • Low startup cost ($200–$500)
  • Infinite product selection
  • Location independent
  • Fast to test products

Cons

  • Lower margins than FBA
  • Supplier quality issues
  • Long shipping times from China
  • Requires advertising budget

FAQ

How much does it cost to start a Shopify dropshipping store? +

Shopify costs from $39/month after the trial. Add $200–$500 for test ads. Total realistic startup: $300–$700 for your first month.
