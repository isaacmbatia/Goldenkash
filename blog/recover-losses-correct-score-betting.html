How to Recover Losses After a Losing Streak in Correct Score Betting

✍️
GoldenKash Editorial Team
March 2026 · 8 min read

Correct score betting is inherently high-risk, high-reward. The odds are massive (often 8.00 to 20.00+), but the strike rate is painfully low. Because of this mathematical reality, experiencing a losing streak of 10 or 15 bets is entirely normal. The danger occurs when panic sets in, leading to bankroll obliteration.

Here is the disciplined approach to recovering your bankroll when you've hit a brutal downswing.

1. Do Not Chase Losses (The Martingale Trap)

The most common and devastating mistake bettors make when down is doubling their stakes to win everything back in one go. This is the Martingale system, and it is a guaranteed path to bankruptcy. If your standard unit size is $10, and you lose 5 bets in a row, pacing a $50 bet on a random 1-1 draw out of sheer desperation will only dig the hole deeper. Accept the loss, and return to flat betting (staking the same amount every time).

2. Implement "Dutching" on Correct Scores

If picking one exact score is proving impossible, shift your strategy to "Dutching." Dutching involves covering multiple correct score outcomes in the same match to guarantee a profit if any of them hit. For example, if you analyze a tight match, you might place proportional bets on 1-0, 0-0, and 1-1. While your profit margin drops, your strike rate increases dramatically, helping rebuild your confidence and bankroll.

3. Briefly Step Down to Lower Variance Markets

If correct scores are destroying your morale, take a one-week break from them entirely. Shift your focus to lower variance markets like Double Chance (1X/X2) or Over 1.5 Goals. The odds are terrible (1.20 to 1.30), but hitting five of these in a row acts as a psychological reset. The goal isn't massive profit here; it is retraining your brain away from the despair of losing.

4. Audit Your Betting History

Pull up your sportsbook account and export the last 30 losing bets into Excel. Look for patterns: Are you losing heavily on Sunday evenings when you're tired? Are you betting on random leagues in South America that you know nothing about? Eliminate the "boredom bets" and your strike rate will naturally recover.
