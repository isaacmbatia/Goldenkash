What Is a Virtual Assistant?

A Virtual Assistant (VA) performs administrative and operational tasks for business owners and executives — remotely, from your home. Tasks range from email management and scheduling to social media, research, customer support, data entry, and content creation. VAs are in massive demand as more businesses shift to remote operations.

Types of Remote Roles

  • General VA: Email, scheduling, research, data entry — great for beginners
  • Executive VA: High-level support for C-suite executives — requires more experience
  • Customer Support Rep: Handle live chat, email tickets, or phone calls for online businesses
  • Social Media VA: Create, schedule, and respond to comments across social platforms
  • Tech VA: WordPress management, basic HTML, CRM setup
  • Real Estate VA: Property research, MLS listings, client communication

FAQ

Do I need previous experience to become a VA? +

Entry-level VA roles often require only basic computer literacy and good communication skills. Many successful VAs started with no prior experience. Free courses on Coursera or YouTube can upskill you quickly in specific VA niches.
