Why Short-Form Video Is the Biggest Opportunity Right Now

TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts collectively see over 5 billion views per day. Platforms are investing massively in creator monetisation. Unlike long-form YouTube (which requires 1,000 subscribers to monetise), short-form creators can earn from as few as 10,000 followers — and sometimes sooner through affiliate links and brand deals.

5 Ways Creators Earn Beyond Creator Funds

1. Affiliate Marketing

Add affiliate links to your bio and mention products in videos. A viral TikTok with 1M views pointing to an Amazon affiliate link can earn $500–$2,000 in commissions in 24 hours.

2. Brand Sponsorships

Brands pay influencers to feature their products. Micro-influencers (10K–100K) earn $100–$2,000 per post. Mega-influencers (1M+) earn $10,000–$100,000+ per post.

3. Digital Products

Sell your own products directly to your audience — presets, courses, ebooks, or templates. A creator with 50K engaged followers can sell $5,000–$20,000 of digital products per launch.

4. Live Gifts

TikTok LIVE and Instagram Live allow followers to send virtual gifts that convert to real money. Top live streamers earn $500–$5,000/day from gifts.

5. User-Generated Content (UGC)

Brands pay creators to produce authentic-style video content for the brand to use in its own advertising — without you even needing to post it. UGC creators earn $100–$500 per video.

How to Grow Your Account Fast

  • Post consistently — 1–3 times per day on TikTok, at least 5 Reels/week on Instagram
  • Find your niche and stick to it — algorithm rewards consistency
  • Hook viewers in the first 1–2 seconds
  • Use trending sounds and relevant hashtags
  • Study your analytics — double down on what performs
  • Engage with your audience in comments to boost reach

FAQ

How many followers do I need to start earning on TikTok? +

You can earn from affiliate links with even 1,000 followers. For the TikTok Creator Rewards Program, you need 10,000 followers and 100,000 views in the last 30 days. Brand deals start happening at 5,000–10,000 followers in a focused niche.
