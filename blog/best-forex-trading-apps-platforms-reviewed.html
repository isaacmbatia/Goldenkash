Best Forex Trading Apps and Platforms Reviewed

🌍 Geographic Note: While these strategies work globally, this guide is highly optimized for readers in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia looking to earn in USD/GBP.
📱
GoldenKash Reviews Desk
March 2026 · 10 min read

The days of needing six monitors to trade forex are over. Over 60% of retail forex trading in 2026 takes place on mobile devices. Let’s break down the best apps and analyzing platforms.

1. MetaTrader 4 and 5 (MT4/MT5)

The undisputed kings of forex execution. While the interface feels like Windows 98, it is bulletproof. Every serious ECN broker supports MT4/MT5. It allows for advanced Expert Advisors (trading robots) and custom indicators.

2. TradingView

This is the greatest charting platform ever made. While it wasn't historically built to execute the actual trades, no professional trader operates without it. You map your analysis on TradingView's beautiful, cloud-synced charts, and execute the trade on MT4.

3. cTrader

The modern rival to MetaTrader. cTrader provides much better order-depth visibility (Level II pricing) and a vastly superior, modern UI immediately out of the box. Highly favored by advanced scalpers.

4. eToro Mobile App

Designed for copy trading. If your primary goal is to allocate funds manually to mirror other profitable investors rather than analyzing raw candle data yourself, the eToro mobile interface is flawless.
