Mobile Car Detailing: How to Start a $2,000/Week Local Side Hustle

✍️
GoldenKash Editorial Team
March 2026 · 9 min read

Most side hustles discussed online are completely saturated screen-based jobs (dropshipping, copywriting). But there is a massive gap in the market for premium, reliable, blue-collar services. People with high incomes will gladly pay massive premiums for convenience. This is why Mobile Car Detailing is currently one of the most lucrative weekend gigs available.

You aren't running a $10 drive-thru wash. You are providing a luxury service where you drive to their house, spend 3 hours on their SUV, and charge $300.

The Startup Kit (Under $500)

You do not need a massive work van with a built-in water tank to start. You can operate out of the trunk of a Honda Civic as long as you plan on using the customer's water hose and power outlet. Your essential kit includes:

  • A commercial-grade Shop-Vac (Wait for a sale at Home Depot).
  • An electric pressure washer ($150).
  • A foam cannon attachment.
  • High-quality microfiber towels, interior plastics cleaner, and ceramic spray wax.

Pricing Your Services

Do not compete on price. If you charge $50, you will attract the worst customers who expect miracles on cars that haven't been cleaned in a decade. Frame yourself as a premium service.

Offer two packages:

  • The Maintenance Wash ($150): Exterior wash, wax, tire shine, and a basic interior vacuum/wipe down. (Takes 1.5 hours).
  • The Deep Clean ($300): Comprehensive interior shampooing, steam cleaning air vents, and pet hair removal. (Takes 3 to 4 hours).

How to Get Premium Clients

Your target demographic is busy parents with high incomes (lawyers, doctors) who drive large SUVs (Suburbans, Escalades) that are constantly wrecked by their toddlers. They have disposable income but zero free time.

To find them, join affluent local neighborhood Facebook Groups (like "Moms of [Wealthy Suburb]"). Post a highly satisfying "Before and After" picture of a minivan interior you cleaned for a friend. The post should say: "Hi neighbors! I do mobile detailing. I come straight to your driveway so you don't have to wait in line at the car wash. I have 3 slots open this weekend for interior deep cleans."

The Upsell: Ceramic Coatings

Once you master basic cleaning, learn how to apply Ceramic Coatings. This is a liquid polymer applied to the exterior that protects the paint for years. The materials cost about $60, but you can charge $800 to $1,200 for the application because it requires intense prep work and skill. Doing one of these a week will instantly double your income.
